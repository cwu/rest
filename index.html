Rest

A better way to sleep

Problem to Solve

Many people today do not have a healthy nights rest. Bad sleep patterns negatively affect a person's daily function, productivity and health.

43% wake up greater than once nightly - Central Michigan University
[bad sleep] leads to 47% higher risk of heart attack - National Post
However there is no easy way to get insight into our sleeping behaviour which both easy to use and non-intrusive.

What is Rest?

Rest is is an affordable device that can accurately measure one's sleep behaviour without interfering with their natural sleeping habits.

How does it Work?

Rest uses actigraphy to detect the sleeping phases. It is 94% accurate with traditional methods such as polysomnography.
